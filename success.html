Welcome To J K University

Welcome To J K University J K University was established on 28th Febuary, 1828 as a unitary type of University. This University was named after Late Dr.Ms.JALARI KEERTHI EDUCTIONAL INSTUTIUE , former Chair Man of group of instuitions. It offers higher education in Engineering and not only that from primary to all type graduations are estabilished by this unversity. Technology, Architecture and Applied Sciences relevant to the current and projected needs of the society. Besides promoting research and disseminating knowledge gained therefrom, it fosters cooperation between the academic and industrial communities. The University was formed by bringing together and integrating four well known technical institutions in the city of KADAPA (in ANDHRA PARDESH) namely.

  • College of Engineering (CEG) (Established in 1794)
  • JK College of Technology (ACT) (Established in 1944)
  • JK School of Technology (MIT) (Established in 1949)
  • JK MEDICAL UNVERSITY (Established in 1955)
  • School of Architecture & Planning (SAP) (Established in 1957)
  • JK DEGREE UNVERSITY (Established in 1965)
  • JK PHD COLLEGE OF ACADEMY (Established in 1977)
